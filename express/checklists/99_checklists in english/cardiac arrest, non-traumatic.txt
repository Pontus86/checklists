Title/1. Roles
Text/
Team 1:
1. Confirm airway patency/intubate
2. Ventilate, follow EtCO2
3. Auscultate lungs during rhythm analysis

Team 2:
1. Ultrasound heart: tamponade, contractions?
2. If rhythm + contractions: pulse check
3. Femoral blood gas
4. Ultrasound groin: proximal DVT?

Team 3:
1. Rhythm analysis
2. Defibrillation / medications?
3. Intraosseous needle humerus PRN



Title/2. Rhythm Analysis
Text/
Minimize interruption of chest compressions

VF/pVT: 360 J (4 J/kg) once

Adrenalin every 4th min:
• Directly if PEA/asystole
• After 3rd def if VF/pVT



Title/3. Reversible Causes?
Text/
READ ALOUD, RESPOND ALOUD

"Is the airway open?"

"Do we have a tension pneumothorax?"

"Do we have tamponade?"

"Do we suspect pulmonary embolism?"

"Do we suspect massive bleed?"

"Do we suspect poisoning?"

"What are the Potassium? Calcium? Glucose?"




Title/4. Specific Measures
Text/
Pulmonary embolism:
• rt-PA 50 mg IV bolus + Heparin 5000 Units IV
• Additional rt-PA 50 mg IV after 15 min
• Consider ECMO
• Consider performing CPR for up to 90 min

Hyperkalemia / hypocalcemia:
• Calcium gluconate 100 mg/ml 30 ml (0.3 ml/kg) IV push

Poisonings:
• Naloxone 2 mg IV if suspected opioid overdose
• NaHCO3 50 mg/ml 200 ml (3 ml/kg) IV if poisoning with a membrane-stabilizing agent
• Calcium (see above) + Glucagon 5 mg IV if poisoning with a beta-blocker / calcium channel antagonist
• Lipid emulsion 20% 100 ml (1.5 ml/kg) IV over 1 min
• ECMO



Title/5. ROSC
Text/
• Noradrenalin infusion? Noradrenalin 1 mg/ml 2 ml in 48 ml saline = 40 µg/ml
Initial infusion 0.1 µg/kg/min. Bolus 5-10 µg

• Titrate supplemental oxygen to SpO2 94-98%

• PCI for patients with post-ROSC EKG showing STEMI and for patients who have required defibrillation for VF/pVT

• Target temperature 36ºC




Title/6. Terminate Resuscitation
Text/
As a general rule:  > 40 min CPR, asystole, no heart contractions

Title/Basic CPR
Text/
Chest compressions:  100-120/min

Ventilation:
• Adult 30:2
• Child 15:2
• Intubated: 10-20/min asynchronous

Rhythm analysis every 2 min




Title/Adrenalin and Amiodarone
Text/

Adrenalin 0.1 mg/ml
• Directly if PEA/asystole
• After 3rd def if VF/pVT
• Adult: 10 ml IV/IO
• Child: 10 microg/kg (1 ml for every 10 kg)

Amiodarone 50 mg/ml
• Adult: 300 mg after 3rd def, 150 mg after 5th def
• Child: 5 mg/kg after 3rd and 5th def
