Title/1. Initial Measures
Text/
1-Dry the infant and assess after 30 seconds

2-If effective breathing, HR > 100 bpm: prevent hypothermia (on mother's chest)

3-Cut umbilical cord after 2-5 min



Title/2. Airway
Text/
• Head in neutral position, towel under shoulders

• No airway suction



Title/3. Breathing
Text/
Ventilate with room air 30-60/min

Confirm effective ventilation/chest wall expansion

If ineffective ventilation: one or several of following:
• Jaw thrust
• Change mask size
• OPA/NPA/LMA
• Laryngoscopy, suction, endotracheal intubation



Title/4. Circulation
Text/
Indication: HR < 60/bpm despite effective ventilation > 1 min

• 3:1 chest compression:ventilation (90:30/min)

• Ventilate with 100% syrgas

• Check heart rate every 30 sec, discontinue compressions if HR > 60 bpm



Title/5. Drugs
Text/
Indication: heart rate remains < 60 bpm

Adrenalin 0.1 mg/ml 0.3 ml via umbilical ven or IO

Ringer's acetate or 0 neg blood 30 ml bolus via umbilical ven or IO

Naloxone 0.4 mg/ml 0.5 ml IM if suspected opioid poisoning

Glucose 50 mg/ml 10 ml via umbilical ven or IO



Title/6. End
Text/
Discontinue ventilation when (effective own breathing + HR > 100 bpm)

Discontinue CPR if > 20 min resuscitation and no signs of life
