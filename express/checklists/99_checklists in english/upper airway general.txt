Title/2. Adrenalin?
Text/
Indication: suspected anaphylaxis, angioedema, airway edema

• Adrenalin 1 mg/ml 0.5 mg IM

• Adrenalin 1 mg/ml 5 mg nebulised



Title/3. Open the Airway
Text/
Choose/try among the following:

• Jawthrust
• NPA/OPA
• LMA
• Suction
• Heimlich/back blows/chest compressions
• Endotracheal intubation

Last resort: crycothyrotomy



Title/4. Confirm Patent Airway
Text/
Ventilate with mask (2-person technique)

Confirm patient airway:
• Chest wall expansion
• Lung auscultation: bilateral breath sounds
• Rising SpO2
• Rising EtO2
