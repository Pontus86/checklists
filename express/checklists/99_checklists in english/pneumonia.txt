Title/1. Oxygen?
Text/
Indication: SpO2 < 90%

• Nasal prongs, face mask or mask with reservoir depending on response to therapy



Title/2. Crystalloid?
Text/
Indication: SBT < 100 mm Hg

• Ringer 500 ml IV bolus



Title/3. NIPPV?
Text/
Indication: acute hypoxemic respiratory failure (Grade 2 recommendation)

Contraindication:  severely impaired consciousness or inability to cooperate; high aspiration risk or inability to clear secretions



Title/4. Endotracheal intubation?
Text/
Indication: inability by other means to maintain oxygenation and ventilation



Title/5. Diagnostic Tests
Text/
• Chest x-ray?
• Blood cultures x 2
• Nasopharyngeal and sputum cultures?
• Urine pneumococcal Ag?
• Urine legionella Ag?
• Sars-CoV-2 PCR?
• Influenza PCR?	
• PCR mycoplasma/chlamydia pneumoniae?



Title/6. Antibiotics
Text/
• Cefotaxime



Title/7. Disposition
Text/
• Out-patient if Pneumonia Severity Index (PSI) Risk Class I, II +/- III (see Dyspnea)
• ICU if ≥ 5 points SMART-COP (≥ 33% risk of requiring intensive respiratory or vasopressor support)
