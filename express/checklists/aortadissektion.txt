Title/1. Opioid
Text/
Indication: pain

Risk: hypotension

• Fentanyl 50 μg/ml 1 μg/kg IV



Title/2. Blood Pressure Reduction
Text/
Indication: reduce SBP to 100 – 120 mm Hg and HR < 60 bpm within 20 min of diagnosis

• Labetalol 5 mg/ml (Trandate®) 20 mg IV initially then 20-80 mg q10 min

• If HR < 60 bpm and SBP > 120 mm Hg: Nitroglycerin 0.4 mg SL every 60 seconds

• If HR > 60 bpm and SBP < 120 mm Hg: Metoprolol 5 mg IV
